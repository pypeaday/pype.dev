Psalm 39:7

And now, O Lord, for what do I wait? My hope is for you.

Every Advent I wish I took it more seriously - each year trying to be slightly more diligent about traditions and raising our daughters to understand more than the material-driven "holiday season".

This year has had some ups and downs, ultimately God has been faithful to me in amazing ways. And if he hadn't blessed me in the ways he has, he'd still be faithful.
