Recap

We're in a tale of 2 kingdoms - Empire vs Shalom

In Genesis 1 we sort of have God making space and telling creation to fill it - ends badly Story continues with mankind making some spaces - tabernacle and temple - and God fills it (so much that the priests literally couldn't enter in - see previous episode)

So maybe we need to be creating space, and expect that God will fill it - we are the temple after all
