# Communication, Conflict, and Forgiveness

## Purpose

To normalize conflict and equip the couple to handle it with humility, clarity, and grace.

## Key Scriptures

James 1:19–20; Proverbs 15:1; Ephesians 4:25–32; Matthew 18:21–35

## Themes

Conflict reveals the heart; it does not create sin.

Patterns of hostility matter more than isolated arguments.

True repentance and forgiveness are essential marital practices.

## Discussion Prompts

What is your instinct under tension: withdrawal, pursuit, defensiveness?

What makes it hard for you to apologize or forgive?

## Practical Emphasis

Listening before responding.

Speaking clearly without accusation.

Addressing issues at appropriate times.

## Mentor Contribution

Share a recurring conflict you have faced and what you learned over time.

Emphasize growth, patience, and the importance of humility.
