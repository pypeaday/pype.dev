## Slash Pages

You might have noticed URLs like `/now`, `/uses`, or `/about`. These are called [slash pages](https://slashpages.net/) — a simple, elegant way to share information about yourself using just a URL.

They're inspired by the "slash" convention popularized by people like Derek Sivers. Instead of having to navigate through menus, you can just share a link like `pype.dev/uses` and people instantly see what tech I use.
